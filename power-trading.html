Glossary

Power (baseload) round-the-clock electricity, $/MWh
MWh megawatt-hour — 1 MW running for 1 hour
Gas (Henry Hub) natural gas fuel, $/MMBtu
Heat rate MMBtu of gas to make 1 MWh. Lower = more efficient. Here 7.5.
Spark spread Power − (heat rate × gas) − running cost. A gas plant's margin.
Peak premium how much more daytime power costs vs the 24h average.
Capacity payment a fee for keeping a plant available, even if it rarely runs.
