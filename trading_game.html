The Trading Sandbox

Welcome to the The Trading Sandbox — an interactive learning space where you can explore the world of trading and risk management through simulation games. Test your decision-making skills, understand market behavior, and learn financial concepts in a fun, hands-on way.

🎯 What You'll Learn

  • Risk Hedging: Discover how to protect portfolios using derivatives and smart diversification.
  • Market Making: Learn how traders provide liquidity by setting bid-ask spreads and managing inventory.
  • Strategic Thinking: Balance profit, cost, and risk under uncertain market events.

Each game is self-contained, browser-based, and designed to give you insight into real-world finance mechanics — no installation or signup needed.
